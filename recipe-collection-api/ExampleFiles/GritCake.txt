Grit Cake|1.Add all ingredients except grits to pot and bring to a simmer. 2.Stirring constantly, pour grits in a steady stream. Continue to stir constantly for 5 minutes until grits start to thicken. 3.Turn heat down as low as possible and cook for 20-25 minutes, stirring occasionally, until grits are cooked. 4.Pour onto half sheet tray with parchment paper. Cool overnight. Cut into squares. Dust with cornstarch.
